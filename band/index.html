Maroon 5 is an American pop rock band from Los Angeles, California Original members Levine, Carmichael, Madden and drummer Ryan Dusick first came together as Kara's Flowers in 1994, while they were still in high school. The album garnered a tepid response, after which the record label dropped the band and the members focused on college.

In 2001, the band emerged as Maroon 5, pursuing a different direction and adding guitarist Valentine The band signed with a subsidiary of J Records, Octone Records, and released their debut album Songs About Jane in June 2002. As the years went on they continued to make different albums. They added four very popular albums which were It Won’t be Soon Before Long in 2007, Hands All Over in 2010, Overexposed in 2012, V in 2014, and Red Pill Blues in 2017.

Band Members

American singer, songwriter and actor was born and raised in Los Angeles, California. Adam is the lead singer for the pop rock back Maroon 5. Levine began his musical career in 1994, when he was apart of Kara’s Flowers. The band split once they made their first album. In 2001, the group came together and added James Valentine to the group and started their new musical chapter as Maroon 5.

Jesse was born in Boulder, Colorado. He was apart of the first band with Adam while he played the guitar. While the band is on tour, their professional photographer is Jesse’s dad, Bob Carmichael. When Kara’s Flower broke up Carmichael went back to college and played the keyboards. Two years later, Levine and Carmichael dropped out and headed back to California.

Madden was born and raised in Austin, Texas. He began playing in high school at the Brentwood School in Los Angeles, playing in garages along with friends Jesse and Adam. Madden was also apart of Kara’s Flowers and once they broke apart he went on to attend classes at UCLA. After he attended college, the group came together again with guitarist James Valentine joining the line-up and Jesse Carmichael focusing on the keyboards.

Valentine played in Lincoln bands: Montag, Kid Quarkstar, Mondello and Happy Dog. In 2000, Happy Dog changed its name to Square and he moved to Los Angeles from Anaheim, California, where he taught private guitar lessons out of his home. Valentine had also played with Reel Big Fish, when Barrett fell down some stairs. Valentine displayed a picture from one Reel Big Fish show he played during Maroon 5's appearance on MTV Cribs. Eventually the members of Square and Kara's Flowers became friends and when Adam Levine, Jesse Carmichael, Mickey Madden and Ryan Dusick began looking for another guitarist, Valentine was approached. The band Kara's Flowers changed their name to Maroon. Personal tensions in Square helped the decision to join the band and shortly afterward, Kara's Flowers became Maroon 5.
